The Equity|Balance toggle determines how the Explorer handles reported metrics. With Equity selected, metrics will include open trades according to their marked-to-market value, and periodic metrics will mark open trades to market at the beginning and end of each period. With Balance selected, metrics will only reflect closed trades.
